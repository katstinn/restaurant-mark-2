About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2018, our little restaurant was created by woodland people, for woodland people. We were stunned to find that while staying in the most beautiful mountain range in the world, a young couple couldn't get a romantic bite to eat! We decided to change that. With a little bit of love, a little bit of whale blubber, and a lot of elbow grease, Luke's Whale Shack and Chicken Emporium was born. We're also proud to be the only restaurant endorsed by Grand Teton National Park!
